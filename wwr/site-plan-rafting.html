Overview

Purpose

Welcome to water rafting, your affordable, yet premier family-friendly outdoor summer experience. Whether this is your first adventure and you're getting your feet wet or your a seasoned rafter looking for your next thrill we have a perfect trip plan for you.

Audience

We are here to serve both the leisurely explorer to the adventure seeking family or groups.
We want our guests to have an exciting experience on our rafts as well as an experience that leaves you passionate about coming back.
Our most popular packages include; families, work parties, bachelor and bachelorette groups and church activities. Book your trip today!
